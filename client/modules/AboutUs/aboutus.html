LITTLE FLOWER ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL

     Little Flower School is a Catholic Institution under the management of the Bishop of Guntur Diocese MostRev.Dr.Bhagyaiah Chinnabathini. The sisters of the Cross of Chavanod from Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu, invited by the Founder of the School, Bishop Mariadas, are here to help the administration of the school. The school was established on 16th June, 1982.

     It is a co-educational institution from K.G. to Class X, open to all children irrespective of caste, creed or race. It is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi. At present the school runs classes from L.K.G. to X.

     The Little Flower school endeavours to draw out the best in pupils, not merely in terms of intellectual formation but in the holistic development of students in terms of moral, social and spirituality.
