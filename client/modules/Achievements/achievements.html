AWARDS & ACHIEVEMENTS

Awards

     The International Institute of Education and Management made a survey and honoured the school “The National Golden Award” for its outstanding achievement over the years.
Little Flower School gives due importance to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Sports play an important role in the development of personality and LFS attaches a great importance for promotion of sports. The students of LFS have been doing exceedingly well at the state level and national level athletic championship by bagging both gold and silver medals.

     Many of our students got medals and best Artist Certificates in drawing and Essay writing in Hope Talent Test

     In International English olympiad students secured many i.e., Gold,Silver and Bronze in all the categories

     From 10 Andra Battalion, 4 Cadets went to Pre RDC Camp which was conducted in Secunderabad.

     Little Flower Students got selected in District Level Cricket Team.

     Our Students showered excellence in Takewando Championship.

     Our Students Supriya of Class VIII got 1st prize from Kalanilayam Vedika.

     M.Sravani of VII class participated in school games swimming state Federation Competitions and got two Silver Medals and was selected for National Level Swimming Competiton

     Our Students 10 Andra Battalion students participated in the NCC Day Celebrations and received prizes from Group Commander Harikrishna Naidu Garu

Achievements X-ICSE Examination.
