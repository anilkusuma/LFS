  • Language Lab

    The school has dedicated full furnished Language lab for the students.
    Total of 24 HP computer systems , head phones and mike connected to Language lab server.
    The Lab is equipped with English Mentor software.
    The Language lab is designed for the students from 3rd Std to 10th Std,
    Where the students practice the English language and enhance their skills in LSRW.
    It contains various modules and exercises in Conversation , Public speaking , Pronunciation , Combination sounds ,
    Word stress , sentence stress , Verb checker , Vocabulary & Fluency.
