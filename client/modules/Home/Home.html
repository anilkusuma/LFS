Principal,LFS Guntur

     LITTLE FLOWER ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL is a Catholic Institution nevertheless, it is opened to all children irrespective of caste, creed and race. It upholds secular and democratic values and firmly believes in universal brotherhood of mankind and the fatherhood of one God.

     The school is named after a great saint of the Catholic Church known as ST. THERESA OF THE CHILD JESUS. Theresa Martin is a young and beautiful French girl born in a small town of Alenco, in France, in the year 1873. From her childhood she was a perfect model of innocence, purity and Love of God. At the age of 15, she became a Carmelite Sister sacrificing all worldly pleasures devoting herself to the service of the Lord. She carried the same exquisite qualities of her childhood into her religious life. She often used to say, “I can do nothing by myself but my Father can do all; I am only a Little Flower and He is the great gardener” showing her great love and trust in God.

     In order that the children of this institution may imbibe the true and everlasting values of innocence, purity and simplicity of St. Theresa of the Child Jesus, the institution has chosen her as the Patroness. The Little Flower will inspire and guide the children to greater heights of innocence, purity and simplicity of Heaven.

Faculity

The school has a team of a well educated, experienced and dedicated staff to impart quality education.
However, the school takes special care to expose the teachers periodically to various seminars.

Extra Curricular

Little Flower not only focuses on academics, but gives equal importance to sports, hobbies and cultural activities.
The school has sufficient space for outdoor games, besides providing space for indoor sports activities.

Facilities

The school has an excellent library with over 7,000 volumes of books, periodicals and magazines.
Students are encouraged to cultivate good reading habits and use the Library as an important centre for learning.

Digital Classrooms

All the class rooms are well equipped with most modern technology.
LFS has access to more than 50,000 teaching and learning materials – High quality 2D & 3D Animations, Real life application videos and assessment material.

Language Labs

LFS has dedicated full furnished Language lab for the students. The Lab is equipped with English Mentor software, which has Spoken English, Listening comprehension, Fluency tutor & Read Write tools for the students to practice.

Our Mission

Today’s world is fast deteriorating in RIGHT VALUES and the youth are misguided and uncertainty of the future overshadows the mind of the youth. Hence our institution stresses on moral and religious instruction classes.

However, our children are prepared to meet the challenges of the fast changing world through instilling self-discipline, high intellectual standards, physical fitness and even community service.

Also our children will be inculcated with a deep sense of patriotism, loyalty to the motherland, values of all culture and the need for practical ethics in public life.
