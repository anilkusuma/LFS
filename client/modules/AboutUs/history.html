HISTORY

History

LFS was established in year 1982, June by Rev.Bishop Maria Das sensing the needs of the people of Guntur.

Here is our story

  • 1982

    On 27th May 1982 a team of sisters of the cross Guntur from Tiruchirapalli.The pioneering sisters faced many hardships and were able to make humble beginning in Guntur with Sr.Agnes Theresa as Prinicipal and Rev.Fr.Arulaiah as correspondent.

  • 1982

    Rev.Sr.Agnes Theres - a retired principal of Anglo Indian School was entrusted with the task of choosing the pattern of education and syllabus to be followed.
    After an intensive study it was decided to have I.C.S.E as pattern of education.

  • 1982

    "Little Flower" is a symbolic name used for St.Therese of Child Jesus to whom the school is dedicated.The Management choose St.Therese as a patroness of this school and named this institution "Little Flower School"
    The motto of the school - Love,Knowledge,Service

  • 1982

    The school was inaugurated on June 18th,1982 by late Bishop Ignatius Mummudi and it was blessed by Bishop Mariadas.
    The school started functioning in a thatched shed starting with KG Classes and I std.

  • 1983

    On 26th November,1982 the foundation stone laid to the new school building by Bishop Maria Das and on 15th June,1983 the opening and Blessing of the new school block was done by Fr.Dominic Savic - Vicar

  • 1983

    For Academic year 1983-84 classes from II to V are introduced.

  • 1984

    On 13th May,1984 Sr.Agnes Theresa, Principal of the school left the on transfer and new Principal Sr.Zelie from Chennai took the charge.

  • 1985

    On 26th June,1985 for academic year 1985-86 classes are extended upto VII with I.C.S.E Pattern

  • 1985

    On 23rd October,1985 The consecration and installation of the Bishop of Guntur Most Rev. Dr. Gali Bali as Manager of the school. He is a Doctor in Canon Law and a biblical scholar, having a specialisation in Scriptures. He has undergone theological and philosophical studies in Rome, Italy, the head of the Roman Catholic Church.

  • 1987

    On 19th March, 1987 School got its affiliation to council of Indian School Certificate Examination (I.C.S.E).

  • 1988

    On 1st March, 1988 the first batch of the school began their ICSE examination. For a short period Sr.Alphose Mascherhas worked as principal of the school (1989-90).

  • 1989

    On 14th June, 1989 Fr.Balaswamy, then secretary to Bishop took charge as the Correspondent of the school. Fr.Balaswamy is the second correspondent of the school.

  • 1990

    On 26th June,1990 Sr.Martina has arrived as fourth Principal of the school.

    On 31st August, 1990 a sorrow filled day - The Lord had chosen Rev.Fr.Arlaiah the first correspondent of the school to enjoy his heavenly bliss.

  • 1995

    On 16th June,1995 Sr.Tresa Kalapurayli succeeded Sr.Martina and took charge as the fifth Principal of the school.

  • 1996

    In September, 1996 Fr.Charles Joseph became the third correspondent of the school

  • 1999

    After completion of four years service, Sr.Tresa handed over the charge to Sr.Alphonse Chungath on 16th June,1999. Sr.Alphonse has taken charge as sixth Principal of the School.Sr.Alphonse rendered her service untill June,2003 and again Sr.Tresa took charge as Principal of the School.

  • 2002

    One of our student Chandana Attended National Youth Leadership Forum on Medicine in Penslyvania Avenue, Washington DC.

    Looking back all the alumni had to say that what they stand now is all due to the Little Flower Foundation."This is the best compliment for us"

  • 2004

    A Prestigious award winning instinct. The Internation Institute of Education and Management made as survey to honour the school and principal for its outstanding achievement and our Little Flower was able to stand high to meet their expectation and bagged National Glodstar Award

  • 2004

    Along with National Glodstar Award, our beloved Principal Sr.Tresa Kalapurayil was honoured for her outstanding achievement in the field of Education with the "Jewel of Indian Award".

  • 2005

    After completing her second tenure, Sr.Tresa handed over the charge to Sr.Lily Grace, the youngest principal ever since the establishment of the school, who is working wonders and trying to take school to soaring heights

    The Young, dynamic and energetic sister has introduced several promoting measures as a part of silver jubilee celebrations.

  • 2012

    Sr.Tresa Kalapurayil took charge as the Principal again for third time during 2012-2014.She is an able administrator in bringing up over all development of the school.

  • 2014

    On 15th June,2014 Sr.Bency Sebastian took charge as Principal and has been continuing her services. She has updated the school facilities as per needs and achieved land marks in school history.

  • 2015

    The achievements of Sr.Bency as follows -
    The school secured cent percenet First classes in board Exams.
    All class rooms were digitalized.
    Total campus is under the eye of principal through CC cameras.
    All class rooms furnished with intercom.
    Introduced House System.

  • 2016

    The school strength reached 3000 students. To Impart Quality education, the school has team of 88 meritorious and dedicated teaching staff,5 able and service minded non-teaching staff and 18 supporting staff.
    Management is steadily moving on the path of excellence in the field of education.

  • In the cource of 33 years - Little Flower is soaring higher and higher.We have the privilege of having and excellent Alumni of students ranging in various professions such as Medicine, engineering, others excelling from IIT'S and many out smarting India in their respective professions.
