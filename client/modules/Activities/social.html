Social Activities

To inculcate humanly nature and kindness into the hearts of students from childhood LFS has a unique club named POSCAP.

POSCAP stands for People Organized Social Community Awareness Program.

Here are some activities conducted by these clubs.

POSCAP conducted Flood Relief Programme,Staff along with Principal Sister visited Sathenapalli and distributed clothes and house hold items to the vicitims of Flood.

Visit to Old Age Home to spread happyness - Along with Principal Sister, Students went to Old Age Home to celebrate Chirstmas along with members of old age home.

Share Happiness Programme - Little sisters visited poor to distribute snacks, fruits to share their happiness and bring smiles on their faces.
