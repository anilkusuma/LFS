VISION

Aim

     Today’s world is fast deteriorating in RIGHT VALUES and the youth are misguided and uncertainty of the future overshadows the mind of the youth. Hence our institution stresses on moral and religious instruction classes.

     However, our children are prepared to meet the challenges of the fast changing world through instilling self-discipline, high intellectual standards, physical fitness and evencommunity service. Also our children will be inculcated with a deep sense of patriotism, loyalty to the motherland, values of all culture and the need for practical ethics in public life.

Vision

     Through the apostolate of education, the Sisters of the Cross, aim at leading the children, youth and women to experience God’s liberating love and truth and thus become channels of God’s goodness in the world. This formation takes place in the contexts of one’s relationship with self, others, world and God, through the integral development of the person, while acquiring knowledge and attitude, we make them consciously aware of the evils that prevail in our society and prepare them to be the leaven for the change of the society.

     Here in this school, we Sisters of The Cross of Chavanod aim at grooming the children to be courageous defenders of truth and justice committed to the cause of liberating themselves and their fellowmen from all evil.

Mission

     Today’s world is fast deteriorating in RIGHT VALUES and the youth are misguided and uncertainty of the future overshadows the mind of the youth. Hence our institution stresses on moral and religious instruction classes. However, our children are prepared to meet the challenges of the fast changing world through instilling self-discipline, high intellectual standards, physical fitness and even community service.

     Also our children will be inculcated with a deep sense of patriotism, loyalty to the motherland, values of all culture and the need for practical ethics in public life.
