ADMISSIONS

          The academic year commences in the month of June every year. The school prospectus together with the necessary forms can be obtained from the school office.
An entrance test will be conducted to the new aspirants from the month of March till May end.

Admissions to K.G

     Application forms for admission to the L.K.G. will be issued to those who produce Government Birth certificate. Kindly note that a child should complete 3 years of age by 31st of August of the same academic year.

      The School conducts an interview – oral & written covering all subjects before a child is admitted to UKG or the higher classes.

Admission to Std. I to X – ICSE

     Registration starts from February. Pupils seeking admission will be tested in English, Maths, Science and either Telugu or Hindi Language. Birth record from the Government and T.C. from the previous school must be produced at the time of admission. However, students who are promoted from U.K.G. and wish to continue in the same school should submit a fresh application.

      The School conducts interview – oral & written test covering all subjects before a child is admitted to the I & II classes. For classes III, IV, V, VI, VII, VIII & IX the students will have to write an entrance test in English, Science, Mathematics and II language to qualify for admission. Admission will be confirmed only after assessing the performance of the candidates in the written test.

Payment of Fees :

     School fees should be paid at Indian Overseas Bank, Ring Road, Bishop Bangla campus, Guntur on school printed challan.

1st  Term fee should be paid on or before 10th June.

2nd Term fee should be paid on or before 10th September.

3rd Term fee should be paid on or before 10th January.


School fee must be paid before the due date.After the due date,  fine will be charged
