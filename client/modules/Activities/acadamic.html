Acadamic Activities

LFS not only teaches in traditional teaching methods but adopts new teaching methods to make learning interesting and fun for students. LFS has two clubs to perform acadamic activities namely SMS & SLA.

SMS stands for Science Maths Social Club
SLA stands for Sports Language Arts.

Here are some activities conducted by these clubs.

Under Science Club Activity, the club members conducted a science fair relating to various topics like chemistry in our daily life, medicinal plants, pollution , Herbarium etc.

Under Maths Club Activity, many projects were displayed in the maths exhibition.

Under Social club activity quiz competition and exhibtion of models was displayed.

Under Language Club activity essay writing, story telling and poem reciting competitions were held.
