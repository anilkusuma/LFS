Extra Curricular Activities

At LFS, we focus on all round development of the student. LFS does not limit student to class rooms and books but encourages every student to participate in all extra curricular activities.

LFS students have excelled and bagged medals in lot of events under many categories like Dance, Karate, Cricket, Music, Rifle Shooting, Swimming and many more.

A Prestigious event of Guntur Devision Girls Zone

At Guntur Devision Girls Competition, LFS students has brought home many medals including Gold and Silver in all the categories.

All the students received medals from Chief Guests of the event- Sri Modugula Venu Gopal Reddy(M.L.A), Sri K.V Srini Vasulu Reddy (D.E.O), Sri M.Ganesh (Secretary School Games Federation), Sri D.Srinivas Rao (NTR Stadium)

State Level School Games

Students of LFS achieved Gold and Silver Medals in 62nd State Level School Games which are held in Podduturu, Kadapa Dist and was selected for National Level Rifle Shooting Which will be held in Hyderabad.
